Welcome to Al Moudira Hotel

A Moorish fever dream on Luxor's quieter west bank, Al Moudira offers a lush, artfully aged jumble of hand-painted hallways, quiet garden nooks, and herb-scented air. The villas are new but already feel ancient (in the best possible way), pitched in a jumble of sugarcane fields, date palms, and donkey brays bellowing from the adjoining farm.

Luxury Escapes in Egypt

Al Qarna , Luxor, 1340304 Egypt
December 18, 2025

Al Moudira sits on a vast patch of farmland on the Nile's west bank, about 20 minutes from the Medinet Habu, the Ramesseum, and Valley of the Kings, but entirely apart from the tour bus and cruise ship crush of central Luxor. A free shuttle leaves for the Luxor souk twice a day, though you'll have to call a taxi (or ask the concierge to arrange one) if you're heading out at a different time. Luxor's international airport is about 40 minutes away.

Most of the suites offer plenty of space to move around, and one of them has been enhanced with wheelchair-accessible features. That said, wheelchair users might have a difficult time navigating the gravel paths that swirl to the villas and main pool.

Villas

Al Qarna , Luxor, 1340304 Egypt
February 22, 2026

Experience unparalleled luxury, comfort and privacy in our spacious villas. Nestled within the extensive cultivated gardens of Al Moudira, our villas offer an exclusive retreat for discerning guests. Each villa opens on to a secluded courtyard with its own private pool surrounded by lush greenery.

SUnique in detail, the villas are filled with antique furnishings, original works of art, historic photographs, traditional Suzanis and other textiles, all discovered by Zeina Aboukheir during her travels around the Middle East. A well-stocked library contains aneclectic selection of books, with many specialist volumes on the history of the region.

Hidden Luxury Destinations You Must Visit

Al Qarna , Luxor, 1340304 Egypt
January 15, 2026

Set Nefru, our 37-metre dahabiya, a traditional Nile sailing boat, is moored nearby and ready for your private charter. One of the few early 20th century boats still in service on the Nile, Set Nefru has recently been lovingly restored. The boat now boasts five luxury cabins, is fully crewed and is available as a private charter for long or short cruises.
